I had my first fateful encounter with code two years ago as a freelance Course Strategist helping clients monetize their knowledge online. Having now completed She Codes Plus, an incredible initiative aiming to address the gender imbalance in the industry, whilst completing concurrent studies in data analytics, I'm eager to build innovative solutions and solve complex programming problems as a software developer.
